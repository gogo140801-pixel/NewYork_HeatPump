Heat Pump Installation Costs in New York

Average Costs in NY (Before Rebates)

The cost to install a heat pump in New York varies widely depending on the size of your home, the existing ductwork, and whether you are installing a ductless mini-split or a central ducted system. On average, homeowners can expect the gross cost to range from:

$5,000 to $20,000+

This is the "sticker price" before applying massive state and federal incentives.

Costs After Rebates

Thanks to the NYS Clean Heat platform and Federal Tax Credits, the actual out-of-pocket cost is often slashed by thousands of dollars. In some cases, partial-home ductless setups can cost as little as a few thousand dollars out-of-pocket after all rebates are applied instantly by a certified contractor.
