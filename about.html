About Our Matching Service

Connecting New York homeowners with trusted local HVAC pros.

Who We Are

NewYorkHeatPump.com is a dedicated, independent matching platform. We understand that finding a reliable, fairly-priced, and rebate-certified HVAC contractor in New York can be overwhelming. That is why we built this free service.

We Don't Install Heat Pumps

It is important to note that we are not an HVAC contracting company. We do not sell equipment or perform installations. Instead, we act as a bridge. When you complete our 30-second quiz, our system analyzes your location and connects you with a curated network of established, highly-rated installation companies local to your specific NY Zip code.

100% Free With No Obligation

Using our platform is completely free. The estimates you receive from our partner contractors come with absolutely zero obligation to purchase. Our goal is simple: empower you to compare options, understand your rebate eligibility, and make the smartest financial decision for your home comfort.
