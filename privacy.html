NewYorkHeatPump.com ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website to connect with local HVAC contractors.

You agree that these professionals may contact you via telephone (including automated dialing systems and pre-recorded messages) or SMS text messages regarding your request, even if your phone number is on a state or national "Do Not Call" list.

NewYorkHeatPump.com is a privately owned commercial matching service. We are not affiliated with the NYS Clean Heat program, the state of New York, or any utility company. Rebate amounts and eligibility are determined by third parties and the installing contractors.

You may opt-out of future communications from us at any time. To opt-out of communications from the specific contractors we matched you with, you must contact them directly.
