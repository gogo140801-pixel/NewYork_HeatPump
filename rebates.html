NYS Clean Heat Rebates

A comprehensive guide to state incentives for heat pump installation in New York.

What is the NYS Clean Heat Program?

The NYS Clean Heat program is a statewide initiative designed to encourage New York residents to transition to energy-efficient heat pump technology. Through partnerships with major utility companies (such as Con Edison, National Grid, NYSEG, and Central Hudson), the program offers substantial rebates that dramatically reduce the upfront cost of installing air-source or ground-source heat pumps.

How Much Can You Save?

Rebates are calculated based on your specific utility provider, the size of your home, and the type of system being installed (partial load vs. full load heating). Many homeowners see rebates ranging from $2,000 to over $10,000.

Federal Tax Credits (IRA)

In addition to New York State rebates, the federal Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) allows homeowners to claim a tax credit of up to 30% of the installation cost (capped at $2,000) for qualifying heat pumps.
